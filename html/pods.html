GLAMPING PODS

We are excited to introduce our brand new glamping pods available from March 2020.
Set in the countryside a short drive from the hotel, we have created a quiet escape to help our guests relax and enjoy the simpler things again.
Have a look below at what you can expect and book your stay with us now!

  • Private hot tub for duration of stay (15.00 – 21.00) (two night stays 10.30 – 21.00)
  • Each lodge can sleep 4 persons
  • Fully glazed gable end to soak up countryside views
  • Fully equipped ensuite
  • Kitchenette (Fridge, kettle, toaster, microwave, sink, cutlery, crockery & glassware
  • Wide screen TV
  • Double bed, plus a double sofa bed (All bedding & towels provided)
  • Use of communal sauna on site/ wi-fi/ games room/ play park
  • Outdoor patio furniture with a charcoal BBQ pit (byo charcoal)
  • Check in 15.00 – 18.00 (early Check in Available of 1pm for an additional £20)
  • Check out 10.30 (late check out of mid day available for an additional £20)
