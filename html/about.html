Our Story

Opening its doors in 1902, Royal Hotel has been providing guests with an excellent stay experience. When first opened the hotel had the same number of rooms, always fully booked because of it's great atmosphere and perfect location. It was the perfect getaway and still aims to be to this day. Continually offering new experiences for our guests we pride ourselves in going the extra mile for their comfort and enjoyment. We pride ourselves on employing pleasant, friendly staff who have a passion for providing great service and going that extra mile.
Over the years we have been awarded many awards and accreditations for our service in the leisure and hospitality sector. We are proud of everything we have achieved and will endeavour to keep up our great name!

What to Expect From Us

Friendly Staff
Our staff are highly trained and devoted to ensuring you have the most pleasant experience with us. All our staff have a passion for the hotel and enjoy working with us as part of our team. We believe that's what makes our hotel so special.
Excellent Service
We strive to provide the best possible service to our guests. Whether you are staying with us or just visiting the restuarant we will ensure that you get the best care while in our company. We make sure that we do everything we can to keep our guests happy.
Great Atmosphere
We have live entertainment for our guests to enjoy when unwinding and relaxing with a meal or drink in our restaurant and bars. We have some of the best local talent playing every night. There is a great atmosphere throughout the rest of the hotel.
